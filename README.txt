Instructions:
click the "Quiz Taker" button. you should see a dropdown menu of which category to take.

you will be given 10 questions. once youve made your selection for a question, there is a "Submit" button to enter your answer.
If correct, you will be given one point. if incorrect, the correct answer will be shown.

At the end of the quiz, you will be shown your final score (out of 10 questions). 
If you would like to attempt another quiz, press the "Back to Main Menu" button.
